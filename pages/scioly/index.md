<h1 class="subpage-title">Science Olympiad</h1>


<div class="text-blurb">
    <p>Science Olympiad is a national tournament where students compete in various science and engineering events. I competed for seven years throughout middle and high school, attending a number of competitions and picking up a few awards along the way. My competitive history is archived <a href="https://scioly.org/wiki/User:Trying" target="_blank">on my SciOly Wiki page</a> for anyone who might be curious what my placements looked like.</p>
    <p>Currently, I volunteer and help organize events with <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/purduescioly/home" target="_blank">Purdue Science Olympiad</a>. I have been secretary of the club for the last two years. For those looking to volunteer or register for Purdue Scioly, go to our website!</p>
</div>

Do not try and trade these for private sets. Second, these tests have been co-authored with fellow test writers and represent shared intellectual property. You can use them as study materials, but please respect our work and do not plagiarize them in any way! Finally, if you have any questions or think that there are any mistakes, feel free to reach out. </p>
