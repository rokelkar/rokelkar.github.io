            <span class="project-date">Summer 2026</span>

My research is focused on developing a program to use the data from the 2D laser scanner to do real-time manufacturing defect detection. I'll be presenting on the work that I've done at the SURF Summer Symposium on July 30th, 2026!</p>

            <span class="project-date">April 2025</span>

We were tasked with creating a digital model of a small piston-driven engine with marginal design documents. Navigating these challenges significantly improved my mastery over Siemens NX.</p>
